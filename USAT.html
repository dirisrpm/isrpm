UTS Signature Self-Assessment

A structured self-assessment against ubiquitous technical surveillance. Work through each practice; your end-user-device signature is scored across the UTS collection vectors, with prioritized actions to reduce it.

This assessment turns disciplined cyber-hygiene practices into a read on your personal digital signature — how observable, identifiable, and correlatable you are to an adversary leveraging ubiquitous technical surveillance (UTS). You'll answer 48 questions across seven practical areas. There are no right answers to perform for; an honest self-check is the only thing that produces a useful result.

Nothing here is saved or sent. This tool runs entirely in your browser. Your answers are held only in memory for this session, are never transmitted to or stored on any server, and disappear when you close the tab. The only output is the report you choose to generate.
